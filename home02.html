Nigeria

Nigeria bears a substantial malaria burden, with high transmission rates across the country, particularly in rural areas. Interventions such as bed nets and antimalarial drugs are crucial for combating the disease.

Mozambique

Malaria is a major public health issue in Mozambique, especially in the central and northern regions. Ongoing initiatives focus on distributing insecticide-treated bed nets and improving access to antimalarial medications.

Uganda

Malaria is endemic in Uganda, and efforts concentrate on expanding preventive measures like indoor residual spraying and promoting prompt and accurate diagnosis and treatment to reduce the disease's impact

Mali

Malaria is a leading cause of morbidity and mortality in Mali, prompting initiatives to enhance healthcare infrastructure, distribute insecticide-treated bed nets, and provide effective antimalarial drugs.

Côte d'Ivoire (Ivory Coast)

Malaria is a significant health concern in Ivory Coast, with emphasis on expanding access to insecticide-treated bed nets and improving community awareness for early diagnosis and treatment.
